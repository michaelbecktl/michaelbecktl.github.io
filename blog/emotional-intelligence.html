Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is related to the set of skills in an individual that relate to emotions. These skills include but are not limited to understanding, managing and then using emotions effectively.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ has more to do with how an individual reacts during certain siuations or when interacting with other individuals, such as communicating with others or when something happens that may affect an individual's emotions. IQ on the other hand relates to knowledge, and information we understand and how well we understanding and use information to make decisions.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is important because how we interact and communicate with others is vital in creating healthy relationships in our daily lives. In workspaces, EQ will also help ensure team synergy and high productivity from people who can work well with each other emotionally.
